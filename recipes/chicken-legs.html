Crispy Baked Chicken Legs

Description

Crispy Baked Chicken Legs inspired by a random recipe website.

Quick and easy method to make chicken legs.

Ingredients

  • chicken legs(duh)
  • 2 tablespoons chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon himalayan pink salt
  • 2 tablespoons tamari

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken legs in a large bowl.
  3. Spread chili powder, garlic powder, cayenne, and salt onto the chicken; pour sauce over top.
  4. Mix thoroughly and transfer to a baking sheet.
  5. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 45 to 50 minutes.
  6. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  7. Your crispy baked chicken legs should now be ready!
